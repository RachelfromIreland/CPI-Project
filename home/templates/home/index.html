Our Facilities

CPI Ltd is a community centre in Castlefinn, Donegal with a lot of various facilities available to members of the community. Our sports hall can be rented by the hour for groups or booked for children's parties. We have bouncing castles available. We also have a computer suite and various meeting rooms that can also be hired by the hour.
